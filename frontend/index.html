Trip Distribution by Zone

Darker zones indicate higher trip volumes. Manhattan dominates with concentrated activity in Midtown and Financial District.

Trips by Hour of Day

Peak demand occurs at 6-7 PM during evening rush hour, with secondary peak at 9 AM. This reveals concentrated office departure times versus dispersed morning commutes.

Average Fare by Hour

Late-night trips (2-5 AM) command 50-67% higher fares due to longer distances and reduced transit alternatives, creating premium pricing opportunities for drivers.

Top 15 Pickup Zones

Airport zones (JFK, LaGuardia) and Manhattan business districts (Midtown, Financial District) generate highest trip volumes, indicating strategic positioning opportunities.

Borough Comparison

Manhattan accounts for 90%+ of trips but has lower fare-per-mile due to short, frequent rides. Outer boroughs show longer distances but lower overall demand.
